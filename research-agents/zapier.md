# Zapier — Research Brief

**Generated:** 2026-03-31

## Company Overview
Zapier is a no-code automation platform that connects over 8,000 apps and AI tools, enabling businesses to build workflows ("Zaps") that automate repetitive tasks across their software stack. The company serves the SMB-to-enterprise market, with 69% of Fortune 1000 companies using Zapier in their tech stacks. Zapier is a privately held, fully remote company that has automated over 81 billion tasks since launch and has expanded aggressively into AI agents and orchestration.
